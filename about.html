About JYM

We are a construction company specialising in roofing, solar panel installation and property maintenance. Our team is made up of 8 highly skilled tradesmen who have experience providing a range of services within the construction industry.

We are a Bournemouth based company BUT operate nationwide. No job too big or small. At JYM we have experience providing a range of services within the construction industry. We offer a range of property maintenance and installation services such as roofing work, pointing, cladding, along with a range of general building services, tree surgery and lawn care.
